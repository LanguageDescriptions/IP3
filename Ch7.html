Longer Analytic Sketches

Below are two two longer languages analyses, designed to show how a linguist would construct a sketch of an aspect of a phonological system. Complete analyses of all of the phonology of either of these languages would require a whole book, what is offered here is coherent subsets of the language, also written in "objective reporting" style, not "textbook explanation" style. Reading actual language descriptions, not textbook problem-solving excerpts, is essential to professional development as a phonologist.
