In collecting these examples, we noticed two special words, ‘to open’ and ‘to give’. Though they both seem to be kuhana, they still do not sound the same. ‘To open’ sounds more like ‘to know’, and ‘to give’ sounds more like ‘to cook’, at least as far as “emphasis” is concerned. We won’t immediately leap to any final conclusions, but this idea of differences in “emphasis” is now starting to accumulate support.
